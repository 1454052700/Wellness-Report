Measurement
What It Is
Weight
Total weight of yourself
Body Mass Index
A measure of body fat when compared to your height and weight
Body Fat Percentage
Total percentage of fatty tissue in your body
Muscle Rate
Total percentage of muscle in your body
Body Water
The total amount of fluid within the body
Bone Mass
Measurement of minerals (calcium and phosphorous) in your body’s bones
Basal Metabolic Rate
The rate at which the body uses energy while at rest to keep vital functions going, such as breathing and keeping warm.
Visceral Fat
The body fat that is stored within the abdominal area surrounding organs
Protein Mass

Deep and Light Sleep
While you sleep, your mind drifts in and out of two key sleep stages, light and deep sleep. WIth light sleep, your body begins to slow and become more rested. Most adults spend about half of their time asleep in light sleep. Deep sleep is the next stage after light sleep where longer periods of sleep that occur in the first half of the night. You will feel refreshed in the morning after a deep sleep.
